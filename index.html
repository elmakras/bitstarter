Hangouts is a new kind of social app that allows you to broadcast your location to people on your Hangout friends list, displayed on a Google map interface.
Dropping a pin on a location on the map allows you to create a Hangout which people can be invited to. When a friend opens Hangout on their device, they will be notified of the created Hangout and its location and can join that hangout either physically, or virtually via the Hangout chat room.

Be social - Whether its getting together play video games, a barbecue at the beach or a night out at the clubs, create a Hangout and have fun hanging with friends and maybe even make new acquaintances.
Eliminate boredom - Don't sit around bored at home. Browse the map to see what Hangouts are happening near you and join in on the fun.
Keep in contact - Follow your friends as they travel around their world, view photos of their intrepid journeys and keep in touch via a virtual Hangouts.

FAQ

Can I choose who I invite to any Hangouts I create?
Of course. Simply select which friends you wish to invite to the hangout when creating the event. The event will only appear on maps of users you have invited.
Can I hide me location from certain Facebook friends?
Sure. Simply click their name on your friends list select [Hide me from this user].
Can I make public Hangouts?
Certainly. Simpliy click the [Public] option when creating your Hangout. Public Hangouts can be rated by other users and if they receive too many down votes then they will default to a private Hangout. As such, limit public Hangouts to Hangouts that really are open to the public. In other words, Hangouts like "NYC Times Square New Years Countdown" are great public Hangouts; however, "Gemma's 13th Birthday" is not.
Isn't this an invasion of my privacy?
Your location is only shared with those on your friends list and is only viewable for the time length specified when clicking the [Put me on the map!] button (for anywhere from 1 minute until 1 week). Furthermore, live location tracking is only available during the specified "Put me on the map!" time length and is only active if you are looking at the app. After the time limit specified, you will no longer appear on any map thus hiding your current location and location history.
